About this Project

This project was created to explore the affordability of housing in select zip codes throughout the United States.
Median sales price information was pulled from Zillows database and affordability scores were calculated using the
National Association of Realtors formula for Housing Affordability.

Housing Affordability Index = (Median Salary / Qualifying Salary) * 100

Where Median Salary was pulled from the Census Bureau's Annual community survey for 2017, with a value of $63,000.
Qualifying Salary was calculated using the Monthly Mortgage Payment of the median sales price in each zip code, with 20% down payment.

This project is powered by a Flask backend, NoSQL database, and D3.js frontend.
